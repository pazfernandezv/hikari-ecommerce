      Who we are

      We are two friends who met while studying Industrial Design and share the same passion for objects that transform spaces. That's how Estudio Hikari was born: a project where design and emotion meet.

      Hikari means "light" in Japanese, and for us it represents much more than a physical element: it is energy, clarity, and that spark that transforms spaces into living places.

      Every lamp we design is the result of a shared process of conversations, sketches, tests and learnings; but above all, of a constant search to create objects that illuminate with soul.
